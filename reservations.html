Considering a reservation at Scarlet Sails Hotel? Please review our reservation policy before making a reservation. If you decide to stay with us, you can make a reservation by email, fax, or telephone. See our Contact Us page for the contact details. Please allow our staff 24 hours to process and confirm email and fax reservations.

Reservation Policy

  • There is a two-night minimum stay required if a Friday night is included.
  • We accept the following methods of payment:
    • Cash
    • Visa
    • American Express
  • Deposits are required for a guaranteed reservation.
  • To receive a full refund, we request a five day cancellation notice.
  • Check-in time is between 1:00 pm and 5:00 pm.
  • Check-out time is 11:00 am.
