Here you'll find some frequently asked questions about our hotel. We hope that you find it interesting and informative. These are the questions that people call our front desk with most often.

Why does your hotel has the name "Scarlet Sails"?

"Scarlet Sails" is a romantic novel by the russian writer Aleksandr Grin. This is the story about a girl from the small seaport town who was told by a kind elder that one day a decent man of her dreams will come to her on a ship with red sails. Years were passing, but she held this expectation in her heart, even though her father told her it was a joke, and everybody had fun on her.
One day, a new guy, a former prince, arrived to the city and fell in love with her. When he heard the story, he bought a ton of red matery, made sails of it, and attached them to his ship he was a captain of. Then he led the ship along the coastline and made a proposal to his beloved one.

This story is a romantic tale about believing in dreams. But "Scarlet Sails" hotel is real! Our guests get high comfortable rooms and high quality service they could even imagine! Book today, welcome to our hotel, and your dreams will come true!!!!

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-out time is 11am and check-in is between 1 and 5pm. Speak to the front desk staff if you have special circumstances.

In addition to your accommodations, what other services do you offer?

The Scarlet Sails Hotel is the perfect place for a romantic getaway, a fully catered business meeting, or an event as special as a wedding. We do offer great room service food as well as a full service spa, both of which help our guests feel like they've come to the right place to get away from it all.

Do you have a pool?

Like all hotels we too have a pool, but ours is actually quite unique. You'll just have to see it to believe it.

Do you accept AAA discounts, or other discount cards?

No we don't accept any discount cards, but that doesn't mean we don't have discount prices on our rooms. We do offer different kinds of specials, some seasonal, some for senior, just for returning guests, etc. Visit the events page on this Web site to see if any special discounts are currently in effect, or just call our reservations desk.
